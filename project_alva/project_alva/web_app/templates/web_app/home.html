The Current Situation

As the number of new buildings and living space square meters in Sofia is on the rise, we offer our clients a unique solution for property value estimation through Machine Learning. We believe that the current ways of performing property assessment are outdated and, in most cases, incorrect. Currently the official approach in the Sofia region based on buying or selling a property is the following:
You ask an unlicensed broker to calculate the value of your property and he/she acts on a hunch and 5 minutes market analysis. There is no official methodology to estimate the value based on predefined specification which means that for different brokers your property value can vary by more than 15%. If you work with one of the big agencies they compare your property with the last deals that they made in your district but this is still highly inaccurate as there are too many time-bonding factors that can influence the price.
When you apply for a mortgage loan from a bank, they are obligated to make the so-called “Official property value estimation from an independent appraiser”. The estimation takes into consideration approximately three properties for sale from the current market and the average square meters price for the district along with several other methods for statistical analysis. This of course is not nearly enough depth of analysis to determine what's best for the client.

The Alva Solution

On the contrary to the current way of working, our algorithm takes into account every property that is currently listed for sale as well as important information in regards to it, such as proximity to parks, metros, whether it's furnished or not and much more. The Machine Learning algorithm calculates the property value using methods and approaches widely used by both S&P 500 companies as well as research centers and universities all over the world. Our results can be traced back, evaluated, and explained. This happens for each individual property value estimation at the moment of your request to our system.
